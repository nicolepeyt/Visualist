COMPOSITION

What is Composition?

Every time you take a photograph, you make intentional decisions about what to include or leave out of the frame. Additionally, you decide how to position the objects within that frame. This is what composition is: the arrangement of elements within the photo. An artists arrangement of these elements can significantly impact how engaging or appealing the photograph is to viewers, influencing its overall success. However, creating a well-composed photograph is challenging. To achieve this, one must consider various compositional elements. Additionally, techniques like the rule of thirds, the golden ratio, and the rule of space are used to guide the composition.
